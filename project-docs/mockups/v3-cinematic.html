Mockup v3 · 5 — Radar in the dark world (mascot decision)
Night-scout Radar. Same rig, two changes: the goggle becomes a glowing cyan lens with a live radar ping, and outlines go deep-navy instead of purple. He appears small — in the watch cards, empty states and email header — not in the hero. The premium look comes from restraint.
The alternative: retire Radar from the customer-facing site entirely and keep a monogram ("FS" in the gold gradient) as the only brand mark. Cleaner, more "$100K agency" — but we lose the personality and the mascot equity from v2, and the alert emails get colder. Decision 03 below.

Sign-off — batched decisions for v3

Reply with e.g. "1B, 2 yes, 3 keep, 4 yes" — one round, then I port to the live site.
